Managing Interactions between Customer's Bank Accounts and Brokerage 
Account  
Title  Managing interactions between customer's bank accounts and their brokerage 
account  
Description  The user can transfer funds between their brokerage account and one of their bank 
accounts.  
Input  Source account number, desired amount, destination account number  
Processing  The user selects one of their bank accounts for withdrawal or deposit. The system checks 
if the source account balance is sufficient. If the balance is sufficient, the transfer 
continues. If the user selects one of their bank accounts as the source, the br okerage 
account is automatically chosen as the destination. If the source is the brokerage account, 
the user selects one of their bank accounts as the destination. The transfer is carried out by 
the bank interface.  
Output  Change in the customer's account balances  
Error 
Handling  If the transfer fails, an appropriate error message is displayed.
